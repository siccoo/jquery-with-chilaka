Alice and Bob work in a beautiful orchard. There are N apple trees in the orchard. The apple trees are arranged in a row and they are numbered from 1 to N. Alice is planning to collect all the apples from K consecutive trees and Bob is planning to collect all the apples from L consecutive trees.
